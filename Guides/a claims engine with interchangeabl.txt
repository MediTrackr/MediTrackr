a claims engine with interchangeable output adapters

MediTrackr is a financial workflow platform built for healthcare professionals to simplify billing, insurance claims, and payment collection.

It acts as a structured financial layer on top of existing clinic systems, helping practitioners convert clinical activity into insurance-ready documentation and track the full revenue cycle without changing their clinical workflow.

The system is built around a modular claim engine supporting public institutions (e.g. RAMQ and federal programs) and private insurers across Canada. Claims are generated from a unified data model and transformed into payer-specific outputs such as structured forms, exportable documents, or fax-ready submissions.

Core capabilities include:

RAMQ card scanning for rapid claim prefill (no long-term storage of scanned images)
Automated generation of billing and insurance claim forms
Component-based support for public and private payers across Canada
Claim submission workflows (fax, export, and future digital integrations)
Real-time tracking of billed amounts, payments received, and outstanding balances
Mobile payment collection via Stripe, allowing healthcare professionals to accept payments directly from their phone

MediTrackr focuses exclusively on the financial layer of healthcare operations — not scheduling, clinical records, or full practice management — enabling seamless integration alongside existing clinic systems.

The goal is to reduce administrative burden, improve billing accuracy, and give healthcare professionals clear, real-time visibility into their revenue cycle.
